Find & Cover — Brown Bear
What this resource is
Find the target picture, then cover every matching picture in the grid. This builds visual scanning, discrimination, and independent work routines.
What’s included
  • 5 differentiated levels (Levels 1–5)
  • Cutout pieces (optional matching extension)
  • Storage labels
  • Color + black & white
Student routine (Find & Cover)
  1. Look at the target picture at the top of the page.
  2. Scan the grid and find every match.
  3. Cover each match using tokens, counters, bingo daubers, or dry-erase.
  4. Check that every match is covered.
  5. Put the page in the “done” area.
Optional extension (Cutout matching)

Alternative use: Use the cutouts to match and place onto each correct square (velcro optional). This is best for small groups or 1:1 support.

Levels at a glance
Level 1 (Errorless): All grid pictures match the target. Success-first.
Level 2 (Distractors): Targets mixed with distractors. Cover all matches.
Level 3 (More Distractors): More items in the field. Increased scanning.
Level 4 (Challenge): Fewer matches among many distractors.
Level 5 (Real Photos): Cover matching real photographs (generalization).
Tips for special education
  • Start easy: Begin at Level 1 until the routine is independent.
  • Prompting: Model “look / find / cover” and fade prompts over time.
  • AAC support: Model core words: find, look, same, more, all done.
  • End on success: Short sessions are fine. Celebrate attempts.
Prep & storage
  • Print on cardstock for durability.
  • Laminate if you want to reuse with dry-erase or tokens.
  • Store cutouts in labeled bags or envelopes.
