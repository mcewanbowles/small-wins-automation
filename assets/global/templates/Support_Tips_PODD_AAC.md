## SUPPORT TIPS (COMMUNICATION FIRST)

This activity is a communication opportunity. Encourage students to use their communication system (PODD, AAC app/device, signs, gestures, pointing).

---

## TOP TIPS FOR SUPPORT STAFF

• **Model communication** while you help (point to PODD/AAC or key words as you speak).

• **Wait 3–5 seconds** after a prompt to give the student time to respond.

• **Prompt least-to-most:** pause → point/gesture → brief cue → model → hands-on only if needed.

• **Reinforce any attempt** to communicate (acknowledge the message, then guide the next step).
