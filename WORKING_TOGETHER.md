# Working Together - Guide for Iterative Design

## How to Nudge / Request Adjustments

### Quick Answer: Just Tell Me What You Want!

I'm here to help with iterative design adjustments. You can "nudge" me anytime by simply describing what you want changed.

---

## Ways to Nudge Me

### 1. Get My Attention
- "@copilot next"
- "@copilot continue"
- "What's next?"
- Any question or request

### 2. Request Design Adjustments

**General Descriptions:**
- "Move the title down a bit"
- "Add more space between boxes"
- "Make the icons larger"
- "The footer is too dark"

**Specific Measurements:**
- "Move accent strip down 0.1 inches"
- "Increase padding by 0.05 inches"
- "Make icons 1.5 inches instead of 1.2"

**Reference Other Products:**
- "Make it match the matching product"
- "Use the same spacing as find-cover"
- "Follow the design specs"

---

## Our Successful Process

### What We've Been Doing (It Works Great!):

1. ✅ You identify an issue: "Accent strip is touching the border"
2. ✅ I fix it and regenerate
3. ✅ You review the result
4. ✅ You give next feedback: "Now center the title"
5. ✅ I fix that too
6. ✅ Repeat until perfect!

**This iterative approach is PERFECT - keep doing it!**
