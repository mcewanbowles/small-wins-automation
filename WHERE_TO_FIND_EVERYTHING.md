## WHAT TO DO NOW:

### Upload to Teachers Pay Teachers:

**Step 1: Download ZIP files**
- From GitHub or your local repository
- Location: `TPT_Products/`

**Step 2: Upload to TPT**
- Go to TPT seller dashboard
- Click "Create New Product"
- Upload a ZIP file (e.g., Brown_Bear_Matching_Level1.zip)
- TPT will automatically extract the 4 files

**Step 3: Set Product Details**
- Title: "Brown Bear Matching - Level 1"
- Description: (describe the activities)
- Price: Your choice
- Categories: Special Education, File Folder Activities
- Grade levels: Pre-K, Kindergarten, 1st, 2nd, Special Ed

**Step 4: Add Preview Images**
- Use thumbnails from `Thumbnails/` folder
- Show 4-6 pages of the product

**Step 5: Publish**
- Review everything
- Click Publish
- Your product is live!

**Repeat for all 7 products!**
