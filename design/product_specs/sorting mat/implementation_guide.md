# IMPLEMENTATION GUIDE: Literacy Sorting Mat System

## Quick Reference: Key Changes from Current Design

### BEFORE (Current Design)
- Generic sorting categories (flies/doesn't fly, swims/doesn't swim)
- Core words scattered around all four edges
- No clear teacher/student orientation
- Single-use categories printed on mat

### AFTER (New Literacy Design)
- Literacy-focused with customizable title area
- Strategic placement: teacher prompts top, student responses bottom
- Clear orientation for shared desk work
- Reusable base + interchangeable category cards

---

## LAYOUT SPECIFICATIONS

### Overall Mat Dimensions
- **Size**: 11" wide × 8.5" tall (landscape, US Letter)
- **Orientation**: Landscape with teacher at TOP, student at BOTTOM
- **Safe Zone**: 0.25" margin for cutting/laminating

### Zone Breakdown (Top to Bottom)

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│  TEACHER ZONE (1.25" tall)                          │
│  [Core words: I THINK | WHAT | WHO | WHERE | SHOW]  │
├─────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│  TITLE CARD AREA (1" tall)                          │
│  [Dashed border box for story-specific cards]       │
├─────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│                                                      │
│  SORTING ZONES (4.5" tall)                          │
│  [2 or 3 columns with category card slots]          │
│                                                      │
├─────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│  STUDENT ZONE (1.25" tall)                          │
│  [Core words: YES | NO | HELP | MORE | FINISHED]    │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

## SPECIFIC MEASUREMENTS FOR 2-WAY MAT

### Teacher Zone (Top 1.25")
- **Height**: 1.25"
- **Background**: Light blue (#E6F2FF)
- **Core Word Boxes**: 
  - 6 boxes across
  - Each box: 1.6" wide × 0.75" tall
  - Spacing: 0.15" between boxes
  - Text: 14pt bold, black, centered

**Core Words (Left to Right)**:
I THINK | WHAT | WHO | SHOW | FIND | TELL

---

### Title Card Area (1" tall)
- **Height**: 1"
- **Width**: 7" (centered, 2" margins on each side)
- **Background**: White with dashed border
- **Border**: 2pt dashed line, dark gray (#666666)
- **Text Placeholder**: "Place Story Title Card Here" (12pt italic, gray)

---

### Sorting Zones (4.5" tall)
**For 2-Way Mat**:
- **2 columns**, each 5" wide × 4.5" tall
- **Gap between columns**: 0.5"
- **Each zone contains**:
  - Category card slot (top): 4" wide × 1" tall, dashed border
  - Sorting area (bottom): 4" wide × 3.3" tall, solid border

**Column Layout**:
```
Column 1 (Left)               Column 2 (Right)
┌─────────────────────┐      ┌─────────────────────┐
│ [Category Card 1]   │      │ [Category Card 2]   │
│ (dashed border)     │      │ (dashed border)     │
├─────────────────────┤      ├─────────────────────┤
│                     │      │                     │
│  SORTING AREA 1     │      │  SORTING AREA 2     │
│  (solid border)     │      │  (solid border)     │
│                     │      │                     │
└─────────────────────┘      └─────────────────────┘
```

**Border Specifications**:
- Category slot: 2pt dashed, medium gray (#999999)
- Sorting area: 3pt solid, dark blue (#003366)
- Corner radius: 0.25" for rounded corners

---

  - 6 boxes across

## PRINTING & PRODUCTION NOTES

### For TPT Digital Product
1. **Save as PDF** (high quality, 300 DPI)
2. **Include editable version** (PowerPoint or Canva link)
3. **Layer names** clearly labeled for customization
4. **Print settings**: 
   - Full page, no margins
   - Color: Full color
   - Paper: Cardstock recommended (110 lb)

### Laminating Instructions
1. Print on cardstock
2. Cut along outer edge (leave 0.25" border)
3. Laminate with 5 mil or 7 mil laminating pouches
4. Cut again for clean edges
5. Round corners optional for safety

### Storage Recommendations
- Mat: Store flat or in large folder
- Category cards: Store in labeled envelope or small box
- Title cards: Store separately by theme/book

---

## ASSEMBLY INSTRUCTIONS FOR USERS

### Setup (< 2 minutes)
1. Place mat on table/desk in landscape orientation
2. Teacher sits at the TOP (blue zone)
3. Student sits at the BOTTOM (green zone)
4. Insert title card into dashed box at top
5. Insert 2-3 category cards into sorting zone slots
6. Place character cards or objects in pile to the side

### During Activity
- Teacher models AAC by pointing to core words while speaking
- Student responds using core words in their zone
- Sort items into appropriate zones
- Discuss choices using modeled language
